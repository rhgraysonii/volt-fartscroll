Volt-Fartscroll

Make your app fart when users scroll

simply add

gem 'volt-fartscroll', :git => 'https://www.github.com/rhgraysonii/volt-fartscroll.git'

And in dependencies.rb

component 'fartscroll'

And it automatically initializes. We are literally cooking with gas out of the box.
